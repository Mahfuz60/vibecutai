# Privacy Policy for VibeCut AI

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025
**Last Updated:** January 1, 2025

## Introduction
VibeCut AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application VibeCut AI (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information:** Name, email address, and profile picture when you create an account or sign in with Google
- **User Content:** Videos, photos, and audio recordings you create or import into the App
- **Payment Information:** Processed securely via Google Play Billing (we do not store payment card details)

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage Data:** Features used, editing sessions, export frequency, AI credits consumed
- **Device Information:** Device model, OS version, unique device identifiers
- **Analytics:** Anonymous usage statistics via Firebase Analytics
- **Crash Reports:** Crash logs via Firebase Crashlytics to improve app stability

## How We Use Your Information
- Provide and improve the App's features
- Process AI video editing requests via secure cloud APIs
- Manage your subscription and in-app purchases
- Send important service notifications
- Personalize your experience with AI music and effect suggestions
- Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

## Data Sharing
We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:
- **Firebase (Google):** Authentication, database, storage, analytics
- **Google Gemini API:** AI video analysis (video content is processed transiently and not stored)
- **Replicate:** AI transcription (audio processed transiently)
- **Google AdMob:** Advertising (for free tier users)
- **Google Play Billing:** Subscription management

## Data Retention
- Account data: Retained until account deletion
- Video projects: Stored locally on your device; cloud backup only with your explicit consent
- Analytics: Retained for 26 months per Firebase defaults

## Your Rights
- **Access/Delete:** Request access to or deletion of your data via Settings > Account > Delete Account
- **Opt-Out:** Disable analytics in Settings > Privacy
- **Export:** Export your data and projects at any time

## Children's Privacy
VibeCut AI is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
